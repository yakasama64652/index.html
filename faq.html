Frequently Asked Questions

How are your virtual assistants trained?
Training starts on day one and ramps up fast. The first 60 days are intense and highly structured, ensuring your VA becomes productive quickly, but it doesn't stop there. Ongoing coaching and real-time support mean your VA keeps leveling up as your agency grows.
What can and can’t a virtual assistant do?
Virtual assistants can handle a wide range of tasks like quoting, follow-ups, data entry, email outreach, and more.

The one line we don’t cross: they can’t do anything that requires a license. That includes binding coverage, discussing policy details, or giving insurance advice.
How do you ensure security and compliance?
We take security seriously. Lava has completed its SOC 2 Type 1 certification and is actively undergoing SOC 2 Type 2. We build systems with compliance in mind to protect your agency and client data every step of the way.
